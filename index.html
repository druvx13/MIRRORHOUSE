The Fracture

"You cannot defeat what you do not understand."

YOUR MISSION

  1. List your 3 weakest mental patterns
  2. Name each one (The Doubter, etc.)
  3. Write a letter from each persona
02

The Rebellion

The Rewrite

  2. Rewrite as The Architect lived it
  3. Visualize the merged version
